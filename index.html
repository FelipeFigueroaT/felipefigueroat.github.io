Stellar Winds of Massive Stars

I've been working with the Massive Stars Group of the UV for approximately two years in determining line and stellar parameters for a set of stars. Also, I've been collaborating with a set of improvements for certain codes to get line parameters for the wind of massive stars, in a auto-consistent way.

Steady States of Dwarf Novae

At the start of the second half of 2020, I was able to join the Dwarf Novae Team, lead by Prof. Dr. Nikolaus Vogt. Our goal is to study the steady state of a dwarf novae, without taking the eruptions using data from Gaia. For that purpose, I develop a method to not only clean the data for each star, but also update the database to the last measure.
