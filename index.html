I'm currently in the Universidad de Valparaíso

I'm doing my undergraduate degree in the Science Faculty of the Universidad de Valparaíso (UV), in Physics but focusing on Astrophysics. My research topics are actually two: the stellar winds of massive stars, specially of OB stars with Prof. Dr. Michel Curé and the study of Be stars, which are B stars that possess a gas disk due to their rapid rotational velocity, with Prof. Dr. Catalina Arcos.

Stellar Winds of OB Stars

I've been working with the Massive Stars Group of the UV for approximately tree years in determining line and stellar parameters for a set of stars. My focus with this topic is obtain a better way to obtain self-consistent line parameters and compare the obtained spectrum with the observed one.

Disk of Be Stars

In late 2021 I had the opportunity to join in a colaboration between ANID (Chilean NAtional Agency for Research and Development) and FAPESP (São Paulo Research Foundation), lead by Prof. Dr. Catalina Arcos and Prof. Dr. Michel Curé, which main goal is to study the disk of Be stars of BeSOS with the HDUST code.
